About Me

Greetings and welcome to my professional portfolio website. My name is Shaghayegh (Sherry) Zarei, and I am a highly motivated individual with a passion for computer programming and web development.

Originally from Iran, I have resided in Canada for five years, during which time I became deeply interested in computer programming. As a result, I decided to pursue an Advanced Diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis at George Brown College, where I am actively expanding my skills and knowledge in this field.

As a web developer, I am focused on creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites that effectively serve the needs of businesses and organizations. My expertise in web development allows me to bring creativity and technical knowledge to every project I work on. I am also committed to keeping up with the latest trends and technologies in the industry to ensure that my skills remain relevant and competitive.

Prior to pursuing a career in computer programming, I earned a Bachelor of Accounting from Qazvin Azad University in Iran. My years of experience working in finance and accounting, both in Iran and Canada, have provided me with valuable skills such as critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and attention to detail.

I am highly motivated to continue developing my skills and experience in computer programming and web development, and I am excited to use my expertise to help businesses and organizations thrive in the digital world. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, and I look forward to collaborating with you in the future.
